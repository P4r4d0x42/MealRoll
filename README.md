# MealRoll

MealRoll is a meal planning app concept focused on reducing decision fatigue, helping people eat out less, and making it easier to commit to simple, nutritious meals at home.

## Concept

MealRoll generates a week of meal ideas, lets users lock the days they like, and reroll the ones they do not. The longer-term vision is to add light gamification so users earn progress by actually following through on planning, shopping, and cooking.

## Core loop

1. Generate a weekly meal plan.
2. Lock meals you want to keep.
3. Reroll meals you do not like.
4. Commit to a plan for the week.
5. Earn progress for following through.

## Early product goals

- Reduce dinner decision fatigue.
- Encourage more meals at home.
- Make planning feel fast and low effort.
- Reward consistency and follow-through.
- Keep the experience simple, modern, and motivating.

## Planned features

- Weekly meal generation
- Lock and reroll by day
- Simple meal categories and filters
- XP, streaks, levels, or quest-style progression
- Completion tracking for planned meals
- Future grocery list support
