Why MealRoll

The idea is to make meal planning feel light instead of draining. MealRoll is built around quick weekly decisions, simple rerolls, and a sense of momentum that helps people keep cooking at home.

Fast weekly planning Build out a week of meal ideas in a few taps instead of rethinking dinner every night.
Flexible, not rigid Keep the meals that feel right, reroll the ones that do not, and shape the week around real life.
Progress that feels earned Gamified follow-through can reward consistency without making the app feel noisy or overdesigned.
