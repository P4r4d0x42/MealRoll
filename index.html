Plan less. Cook more.

Meals for the week without the mental load.

MealRoll helps you generate a week of meals, lock the ones you like, reroll the ones you do not, and build momentum by actually following through. The goal is simple: fewer takeout defaults, easier home meals, and less decision fatigue at the end of the day.
