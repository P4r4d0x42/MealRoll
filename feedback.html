Share the features you would actually want.

This feedback page is focused on potential user wants, missing pieces, and ideas that would make MealRoll more useful in real life. The goal is to collect the kinds of suggestions that help shape the product early.

What would make this genuinely useful?Think about features, workflows, or shortcuts that would help you plan meals and follow through more consistently.
What would keep you coming back?That might be better rerolls, grocery support, difficulty levels, budget filters, nutrition goals, or a stronger progress system.
What feels missing right now?Share anything that would make the concept clearer, easier, or more motivating for everyday use.

Short, practical feedback is perfect. Clear user wants are more valuable here than polished essays.
